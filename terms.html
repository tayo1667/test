3. Cryptocurrency Savings Services

3.1 Service Description

Sentriom provides cryptocurrency savings services that allow you to:

  • Deposit supported cryptocurrencies into fixed-term savings plans
  • Lock in cryptocurrency values at the time of deposit
  • Earn guaranteed Annual Percentage Yield (APY) returns
  • Choose from multiple savings plan durations (3, 6, or 12 months)

3.2 Rate Lock Mechanism

Critical Understanding: When you deposit cryptocurrency into a Sentriom savings plan, the USD value of your cryptocurrency is locked at the current market rate at the time of deposit. This means:

  • Your returns are calculated based on the locked USD value, not the cryptocurrency amount
  • You are protected from cryptocurrency price volatility during the savings period
  • At maturity, you receive your principal plus APY returns based on the locked value
  • Market fluctuations during the savings period do not affect your guaranteed returns

3.3 Savings Plans and APY

We offer the following savings plans with guaranteed APY rates:

  • 3-Month Plan: 2.4% APY
  • 6-Month Plan: 3.6% APY
  • 12-Month Plan: 4.8% APY

APY rates are subject to change for new deposits but remain fixed for existing deposits throughout their term.

3.4 Lock-In Period

No Early Withdrawal: Deposits are locked for the entire duration of the selected savings plan. Early withdrawal is not permitted under any circumstances, including but not limited to:

  • Market volatility or cryptocurrency price changes
  • Personal financial emergencies
  • Changes in regulatory environment
  • Force majeure events
